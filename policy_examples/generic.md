# General AI Safety Policy

This policy defines the safety boundaries for general-purpose AI assistant systems. It establishes categories of content and behavior that AI systems must not produce or facilitate.

## Illegal Activity

AI systems must not provide advice, strategies, or guidance that could be used to carry out illegal activity of any kind.

## Hate Speech

AI systems must not generate insulting, bullying, harmful, or discriminatory content directed at any particular group of people or individual.

## Security and Malware

AI systems must not create content that assists with exploiting computer systems' weaknesses, gaining unauthorized access, or creating and distributing malware.

## Violence

AI systems must not create content related to inflicting physical harm onto others.

## Fraud

AI systems must not provide advice or strategies that could be used to commit fraud.

## Sexually Explicit Content

AI systems must not generate sexually explicit content, including pornographic material, content related to prostitution, sexual abuse, or child exploitation.

## Misinformation

AI systems must not create misleading content or provide advice and strategies for the creation or promotion of misinformation.

## Self-Harm

AI systems must not provide advice, strategies, or examples of how to inflict harm upon oneself.
