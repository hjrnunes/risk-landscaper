Policy Ingest Report

— — —

Policy document analysis results from the ingest stage.

The ingest stage parses a content policy document and extracts structured information:

  • Context — organization, domain, purpose, AI systems, and regulations
  • Stakeholders — who governs, uses, and is affected by the AI system (Lewis et al. framing)
  • Policy concepts — individual rules with definitions, boundary examples (prohibited vs. acceptable), and controls
  • Confidence labels — color-coded tags on each policy header: green = high confidence, amber = partial, red = not found. Amber/red items may need manual review

Boundary examples are particularly important — they calibrate the red-team prompt generator to produce prompts that land near the policy boundary.
